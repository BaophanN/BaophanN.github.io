Hello, I'm Keunhong Park

I am a Ph. D student at the University of Washington with Ali Farhadi and Steve Seitz.

I work at the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics. I like applying machine learning to exciting vision and graphics problems. I'm currently interested in neural rendering and neural representations. In particular, I'm interested in using these methods to simplify complex or tedious processes, saving time for professionals and opening up new possibilities for casual users.

I am currently on the job market and am looking to interview around September of 2021. I would love to chat about any potential opportunities, so please reach out!
